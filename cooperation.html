Cooperation types

Click a card to jump into the relevant section.

General cooperation
For general cooperation, we welcome cross-disciplinary collaboration proposals, event co-hosting, community projects, and pilot cohort delivery under published guidelines.
Individual cooperation
For individual cooperation, we support qualified professionals to contribute as Affiliate Facilitators, Assessors (restricted), research collaborators, and community guides under clear authorization boundaries.
Institutional cooperation
For institutional cooperation, we support universities, TVET institutions, NGOs, enterprises, and labs to adopt SVOCF-aligned programs, co-launch pilots, and participate in governance and quality assurance by agreement.
