Certification

SVOS: a certification for meta-cognition & meta-abilities

A structured model: definition → level descriptors → evidence requirements → rubrics → progression. Designed for future societies where adaptability and sustainable self-development are core.

Core constructs

  • Meta-cognition: monitoring, calibration, reflective learning control
  • Self-regulation: emotion/attention control and sustainable habits
  • Systems reasoning: causality, trade-offs, boundaries
  • Strategic capability: goals, scenarios, execution loops
  • Collaborative agency: communication, trust, co-creation

Assessment logic

  • Artifacts: plans, scenario outputs, decision records, portfolios
  • Reflection logs: checkpointed meta-cognitive narratives
  • Rubrics: transparent criteria and evidence thresholds
  • Verification: peer/mentor/partner validation where applicable

Levels (example descriptors)

You can refine names and descriptors in Annex A; this is a clean starting structure.

L1 Foundation

Stable routines, basic reflective checkpoints, initial evidence portfolio.

L2 Practitioner

Transfer across contexts, scenario-based planning, verifiable execution evidence.

L3 Facilitator

Guides others, improves rubrics, contributes to ecosystem quality and recognition.
