Transparency

Governance, verification principles, and public documents

GLIRA maintains clear scope, verification principles, and a quality assurance loop for partners and programmes. This page summarises governance and provides public-facing documents for orientation.

Governance baseline

Scope & authorisation

Partner activities operate within defined scope. Credential issuance and accreditation decisions remain subject to GLIRA verification and QA.

Verification & audit loop

Evidence is reviewed through checkpoints. Working nodes and accredited providers are subject to sampling, review, and periodic renewal.

Safeguarding & ethics

Safeguarding-first for youth and programmes. Minimal data handling, consent requirements, and clear complaints & escalation pathways.

Complaints & appeals

For complaints, appeals, or safeguarding concerns, contact contact@glira.cc. Use the subject line “Appeal / Complaint — [Country]” and include a concise factual summary and evidence references.
