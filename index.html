About GLIRA

An international alliance for evidence-based certification and accreditation.

GLIRA aligns learning, practice, and recognition through transparent standards, verifiable evidence, and cross-border cooperation. Our work focuses on two pathways: SVOC personal competency certification and Study Tour programme accreditation.

How recognition works

Membership → Candidate → Certified

Membership supports standards orientation and evidence tooling. Certification is optional and subject to verification as a Certification Member (Candidate) through evidence submission and review.

Membership structure
Accreditation for programmes

Study tours and applied learning programmes are accredited based on programme design, safeguarding, risk, evidence collection, and QA cycles. Accreditation requires periodic review and improvement reporting.

GLIRA

GLIRA is a collaboration-focused certification and accreditation alliance. Membership supports standards orientation, evidence tooling, and cross-border coordination. Certification and accreditation are subject to verification and quality assurance.
