About GLIRA

An international alliance for evidence-based certification and accreditation.

GLIRA aligns learning, practice, and recognition through transparent standards, verifiable evidence, and cross-border cooperation. Two pathways anchor the ecosystem: SVOCF (personal competency certification) and Study Tour (programme accreditation).

SVOCF

Three tracks (General / Professional / Youth) and progressive levels supported by an evidence spine.

Open SVOCF
Study Tour

Accreditation for study tours and applied learning programmes with safeguarding-first QA.

Partners & Nodes
