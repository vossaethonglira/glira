About GLIRA

An international alliance for evidence-based certification and accreditation.

GLIRA aligns learning, practice, and recognition through transparent standards, verifiable evidence, and cross-border cooperation. Two pathways anchor the ecosystem: SVOCF (personal competency certification) and Study Tour (programme accreditation).

Certification
SVOCF
Three tracks (General / Professional / Youth) with progressive levels and an evidence spine.
Programme
Study Tour
Accreditation for applied-learning programmes with safeguarding-first QA.
Network
Partnership
Open collaboration. Local nodes for liaison, delivery, and quality operations.

Roadmap (Planned)

Turning commitments into verifiable phases: standards orientation → pilots → QA loop → recognition mapping → digital coordination.

Phase 1
Standards & pilots
Public standards package, partner onboarding, initial pilots.
Phase 2
Evidence & QA
Evidence tooling, sampling rules, QA/audit cycle, partner listing.
Phase 3
Digital coordination
Member portal (planned): portfolios, coordination, activity initiation.
