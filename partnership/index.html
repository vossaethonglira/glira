Partnership

Build the evidence ecosystem together

Partnership is about collaboration mechanisms—pilots, mapping, and co-development—rather than only listing members. You can add a “Partners” page later if you want a public partner directory.

Who we work with

  • Universities / research groups
  • TVET institutions & qualification bodies
  • NGOs & community organizations
  • Industry partners for real-world validation

Partnership modes

  • Pilot: run programs that generate evidence portfolios
  • Co-development: descriptors, rubrics, assessment tasks
  • Mapping: recognition and mutual alignment pathways
  • Publication: joint reports, annexes, and methodology guides

Typical collaboration flow

Step 1 Alignment

Scope, outcomes, evidence standards, and roles.

Step 2 Pilot

Implement a short cycle with rubric-based evaluation.

Step 3 Recognition

Mapping and publication for scaling and portability.
