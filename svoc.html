SVOC Certifications

Symbiotic Value Orientation Competency (SVOC)

SVOC defines competency in value orientation and sustainable action across contexts. The framework is organised into three personal participation tracks and progressive certification levels supported by an evidence spine.

Become a Member Standards & verification

Three tracks

General

For lifelong learners and citizens building orientation and practice across life contexts.

Professional

For practitioners applying SVOC in work settings with role-based evidence and QA alignment.

Youth

For youth development pathways with safeguarding-first requirements and responsible sponsorship.

Levels and evidence spine

L0 is an entry stage (orientation / participation) to lower barriers. Certification levels L1–L3 require verifiable evidence.

LevelFocusMinimum evidence (examples)
L0 Entry Orientation & participation (non-certifying or participation statement) Learning log, basic reflection notes, participation record
L1 Foundation Core concepts and foundational practice Structured reflections, guided tasks, baseline portfolio artefacts
L2 Practitioner Contextual application and project evidence Project outputs, peer/mentor confirmation, impact narrative + artefacts
L3 Facilitator Leading and enabling others; systems-level design Programme design artefacts, facilitation evidence, QA records, improvement loop
Evidence spine

Portfolio templates + rubrics + verification checkpoints + audit trail. This structure enables review, renewal, and cross-border mapping.

Credential lifecycle

Issuance is subject to verification. Renewal and recertification follow defined evidence requirements and quality assurance processes.

GLIRA

GLIRA is a collaboration-focused certification and accreditation alliance. Membership supports standards orientation, evidence tooling, and cross-border coordination. Certification and accreditation are subject to verification and quality assurance.
