SVOCF Overview

Symbiotic Value Orientation Competency Framework (SVOCF)

SVOCF is GLIRA’s personal competency certification pathway. It defines progressive levels and evidence requirements across three tracks—General, Professional, and Youth—so that growth is recognisable, auditable, and portable across contexts. This page provides an orientation and the primary entry points for the three tracks.

Tracks
General / Professional / Youth — aligned levels with evidence spine.
Levels
Progression logic and assessment integrity principles.
Downloads
Framework PDFs and implementation packs (Transparency).
SVOCF — General
Entry pathway for individuals pursuing structured life-orientation and evidence-based growth.
SVOCF — Professional
Professional pathway for facilitators and practitioners with role scope and verification rules.
SVOCF — Youth
Youth development pathway with safeguarding-first design and age-appropriate evidence rules.
Levels & Evidence

SVOCF uses progressive levels supported by an evidence spine. Each level defines minimum capability expectations, acceptable evidence types, and verification logic. Detailed standards are listed under Transparency → 认证与培训.
