Membership

GLIRA Members

Membership supports standards orientation, pathway guidance, and evidence tooling. Certification is optional and requires verification as a Certification Member (Candidate).

Digital member portal (planned): GLIRA is developing a digital portal for onboarding, evidence portfolios, and programme coordination. Until release, services are delivered via official email and partner-supported processes.

Choose your personal track

General member

Orientation and practice across life contexts; recommended SVOC General pathway.

Professional member

Role-based practice and organisational alignment; recommended SVOC Professional pathway.

Youth track member

Safeguarding-first participation, typically with guardian/school sponsorship.

Member vs Certification Member (Candidate)

StatusWhat you receiveWhat it is not
GLIRA Member Standards orientation, pathway guidance, starter evidence toolkit, briefings & updates, member ID (not certified). Not a certification or licence; no guarantee of credential issuance.
Certification Member (Candidate) Submission window, controlled rubrics, verification checkpoints, review outcome report, eligibility for SVOC credential issuance. Not automatic approval; certification remains subject to verification and QA.
How to join

Email us with your track preference (General/Professional/Youth), your context, and your intended level (L0/L1 to start).

Youth safeguarding note

Youth participation and candidate verification require safeguarding and consent conditions. GLIRA prioritises duty of care, minimal data handling, and clear escalation/complaints pathways.
