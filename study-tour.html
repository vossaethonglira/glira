Study Tour Overview

Study Tour Accreditation

Study Tour is GLIRA’s programme accreditation pathway for field-based and applied learning experiences. It emphasises safeguarding, duty-of-care, evidence integrity, and quality assurance so that experiential programmes can be trusted by participants and partners.

Accreditation Flow
Orientation → evidence submission → QA review → listing → improvement cycle.
Safeguarding
Participant protection and duty-of-care as non-negotiable baseline.
Downloads
Policies, checklists, and document packs (Transparency).
Accreditation Flow

Programme providers submit evidence against defined standards (planning, delivery, safeguarding, outcomes, and governance). GLIRA applies sampling rules and a QA/audit loop, with improvement actions where needed.

Safeguarding-first

Study Tour accreditation prioritises safeguarding, transparency, and responsible communications. Providers must show clear risk controls, incident handling, and participant support procedures.
